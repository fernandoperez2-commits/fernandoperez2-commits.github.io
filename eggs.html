Lets make Eggs!

Here's how you make the perfect eggs !

First take three eggs and crack two whole in a bowl and one egg white into a bowl.

Then add about a table spoon of milk in the bowl and whisk

Now add the eggs into a pan with a bit of butter, that is on medium heat

now just constantly stir the eggs until nice and firm, but do not let them brown

Now get ready to enjoy by adding cracked pepper and salt, and even adding ontop of some toast.
